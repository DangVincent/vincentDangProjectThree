dice blackjack

Instructions

welcome to dice blackJack

  1. the objective of this game is to get a count of 21 or a higher score than the dealer, without going over 21.

  2. your score will added by a number shown on the dice between 1 to 6.

  3. you will have two buttons to click on after the game has begun, one button will roll a new dice and the other will keep your current score.

  4. click the start button to begin.

  5. have fun!!! :)
